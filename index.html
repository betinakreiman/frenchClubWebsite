About Us

The French Club is a wonderful community of students who share a passion for the language and the culture of French speaking nations. We strive to expand our culture on French things especially when it comes to food. We collaborate with other clubs and chat with Monsieur Jobe to create a safe, fun, and French environment. We'd love to see you at a club meeting. Au revoir!

  • Cooking in kitchen in international café
  • Watch French movies
  • Eat French food (bread, nutella, crêpes, cheese, crackers)
  • Going to French restaurants
  • Play sports (E.g. soccer, tennis)
  • Cabot Park Village (Senior Center). Teaching them French!
  • Fundraisers (bake sales ex. cookies)
